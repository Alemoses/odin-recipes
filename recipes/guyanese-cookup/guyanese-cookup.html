Guyanese Cook-Up Rice

A bowl of Guyanese Cook-Up Rice

Cook-Up Rice, a Guyanese rice & peas meal, is a terrific one-pot dish that's packed with flavor! You can customize it with any type of peas, meat, and veggies you have on hand, and you'll have a hearty meal ready to go in about 40 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 Scotch Bonnet Pepper(Optional)
  • 6 bone-in chicken pieces
  • 2 skinless, bone-in chicken breast halves
  • 1 TBL olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups uncooked long-grain white rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can(14 ounces) coconut milk
  • 4 sprigs thyme, chopped
  • 1 can(15 ounce) black eyed peas
  • 4 green onions, coarsly chopped
  • 1/2 head cabbage, cored and cut into large chunks
  • 2 TBL butter
  • salt and pepper to taste
