Chicken Curry

A bowl of Chicken Curry

Guyana's beloved comfort food, chicken curry was introduced to the carribbean nation by indentured servants from India in the mid 1800s. Chicken Curry is generally made by simmering medium to small cut up pieces of chicken breasts as well as drumsticks or thighs in a sauce of blended spices. Potatos are added to the curry at the time chicken is cooked. The Potatos are then cooked down until they they are soft an infused with the flavor of the curry.

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken drumsticks
  • 3 boneless chicken breats
  • 3 large red potatos
  • 1 medium onion
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 3 TBL canola oil
  • 3 TBL butter
  • 4 TBL curry powder
  • 2 TBL corriander
  • 1 TBl cumin
  • 1 tsp tumeric
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 pinch dried thyme
  • 1/3 cup water
  • salt
